## Community Work

Deyika was deeply involved in Hulme's community life. People from the area regularly sought him out for meetings and support. He stood as a candidate to represent Hulme as a local councillor.

## Activism and Politics

- Founded the **Mbari Group**, an arts organisation
- Chaired **Manchester Green Party**
- Joined the Green Party in 2011
- Was the **Green Party candidate for Greater Manchester Mayor** in 2017
- Collaborated on **"Say it Loud"**, a festival combining arts and politics
- Worked with the **Manchester Environmental Network**

## Poetry

Deyika was a respected poet whose work explored themes of identity, environment, and community. He performed at events across Manchester and beyond.

## Legacy

Deyika died unexpectedly in December 2017 while campaigning for Greater Manchester Mayor. His sudden passing was mourned across Manchester's activist community.

A fundraising drive was organised to help pay for his funeral, reflecting the love and respect the community held for him.
